## All-Time Favorites

- **The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt** — Open-world RPG by CD Projekt Red. Loved the storytelling, world-building, and the depth of side quests.
- **Dark Souls / Elden Ring** — FromSoftware's challenging action RPGs. Appreciates the tight combat design and environmental storytelling.
- **Minecraft** — The creative freedom and emergent gameplay are endlessly inspiring from a game design perspective.
- **Hollow Knight** — A masterclass in Metroidvania design. Great atmosphere, tight controls, and incredible art for an indie title.
- **Stardew Valley** — Proof that a solo developer can create a beloved, polished game. Inspires Zohaib as a game dev.
